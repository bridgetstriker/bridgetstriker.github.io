How to make a peanut buttter and jelly sandwich

Something to listen to while making (and eating) your PB&J --

  1. Get two slices (or one slice) of your preferred bread
  2. Take the peanut butter from the cupboard
  3. Grab the jelly from the refrigerator
  4. Spread the PB&J on either (or both) sides of bread, in no particular order
  5. EAT!
