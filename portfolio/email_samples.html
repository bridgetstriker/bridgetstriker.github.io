Gender Health Center

At GHC, we’ve been providing counseling and healthcare to the trans and nonbinary communities for nearly twenty years. We are your place for support in Sacramento and Northern California.

Need food? Clothes? How about a place to rest for a couple of hours? GHC is the place to hang, eat, and socialize!

SAFE Credit Union Performing Arts Center

We are just part of the SAFE Credit Union Convention and Performing Art District in downtown Sacramento! And between touring Broadway shows, the Sacramento Philharmonic and Ballet, and our Speakers Series, you’ll have much to wonder at in our remodeled buildings.

We have a long history of putting on concerts, musicals, and sporting events — and there’s so much to look forward to, like Tori Amos at the end of August!

And the SAFE Credit Union Convention Center regularly hosts conventions like ComicCon, high school competitions, and other such events. So add the Convention Center, Performing Art Center, and Memorial Auditorium to your calendar!
